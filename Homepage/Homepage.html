ABOUT US

Our Mission


At E'Connect, our mission is to provide timely and reliable information about emergency services and common household services to our community. We aim to ensure that individuals can easily access the assistance they need during critical situations.

Who are we


E'Connect is a dedicated platform that aggregates information about emergency services such as police, hospitals, and fire departments, as well as common household services like plumbing, gardening, and electrician services. We strive to be a one-stop destination for individuals seeking assistance in emergencies or requiring routine household services.

Our team

Our team at E'Connect is composed of dedicated individuals who are passionate about serving the community and ensuring the safety and well-being of our users. From web developers to content creators, each member plays a vital role in maintaining and enhancing our platform to better serve our users.
